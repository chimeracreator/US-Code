TITLE 34—NAVY [REPEALED]

  Title 34 was repealed generally by act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, 70A Stat. 1, which revised and codified the statutory provisions that related to the Army, Navy, Air Force, and Marine Corps, and enacted those provisions into law as Title 10, Armed Forces.
For distribution of provisions of former Title 34 in Title 10, see Table II set out preceding the text of Title 10.
